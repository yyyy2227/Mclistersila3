STAND ALONE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL - TIS

          

DESCRIPTION:A stand-alone school in the context of Philippine education refers to a Senior High School (SHS) that is situated on a separate site from an existing Junior High School (JHS) or Integrated School (IS), and has its own distinct school administration. In essence, it's a school dedicated solely to Grades 11 and 12, operating independently from other school levels. Here's a more detailed breakdown: Separate Location: Aan existing JHS or IS and shares the same School Head.
